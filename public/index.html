Informed Consent

Please read the following information carefully before participating.

Study Information

Purpose: This study investigates cognitive performance using validated computerised assessments. Your participation will contribute to research on attention and working memory.

What You Will Be Asked to Do

You will complete two short computerised cognitive tests:

  • Continuous Performance Test (CCPT) — approximately 5–8 minutes. You will respond to target letters displayed on screen.
  • N-Back Working Memory Test — approximately 5–7 minutes. You will identify repeated positions in a grid sequence.

Each test includes a brief practice session before the main test begins.

Risks and Benefits

There are no known risks beyond mild mental fatigue. Your participation may directly benefit the scientific understanding of cognitive assessment and indirectly benefit future research participants.

Confidentiality

Your data are stored securely using your participant number only — no names or personal identifiers are collected. Data are accessible only to authorised members of the research team. Results may be published in aggregate, anonymised form.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time by closing your browser window. Withdrawal will not affect your relationship with the research team or any benefits to which you are entitled.

Contact

If you have questions about this study, please contact the researcher using the information provided in your study invitation email.

Data Retention

Your anonymised data will be retained for a minimum of 5 years in accordance with institutional research data management policy.
